My Hobbies

Cooking

While I ultimately decided against a career in Culinary Arts, I have continued to enjoy craft and its creative challenges. I have a signature dish for each of the major holidays ranging from the staples like Mac and Cheese to fall of the bone ribs with homemade BBQ. During warmer months I enjoy growing my own vegetables. They never taste better than when you went out back and plucked them of the plant yourself.

3d Printing

This is a hobby I got into because I wanted to start painting miniatures and playing table top RPGs. I have yet to find a consistant group for game night but I am always ready for adventure. It surprised me how many useful things can be printed. From shelving to camera stands, 3d printing has proven to be more than toys and games.
